About Us

Organic Meat To Meat Lovers

Knowing origin of meat and it's source is as important as eating the meat. We take pride in saying the meat we supply originates from our inhouse farm spread over 12 acres. The sheep and goats are reared amidst this pristine farmland in an organic way. Our livestock is fed with organic crops like napier, maize corn, finger millet (ragi) grass grown in our farmland. The lambs we choose for meat is between 8 to 10 months old to savor the most tender and tasty meat. The mutton/meat is exclusively from male sheep (TAGARU) and male goats (VAATHA). Creating a direct farm to fork concept is our endeavour. We plan to achieve this through an integrated model which includes inhouse livestock rearing, slaughterhouse and meat supply to cater to the meat market/industry. We serve only fresh mutton/meat, no preservatives or stored meat, the freshest with all the juices and goodness intact. All this at an economical price.
